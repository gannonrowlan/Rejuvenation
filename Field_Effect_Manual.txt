#-------------------------------- 5. Chess Board [CB] --------------------------------#
"Opening variation set."
############################################################################################
(Passive effects by the field.):
*   Each Pokemon is assigned a single Chess Piece role based on certain criteria, in the following order:
	*The last Pokemon in the party is the Queen (there may only be one Queen per party)
	*All pokemon send out on the first turn are Pawns (except the queen)
	*A Pokemon holding a king's rock or the Pokemon with the least HP in the party is the King (there may only be one king per party)
	*Pokemon whose highest stat is their Speed are Knights
	*Pokemon whose highest stat is either Attack or Special Attack are Bishops
	*Pokemon whose highest stat is either Defense or Special Defense are Rooks
*   Queens boost Defense and Special Defense on entry and deal x1.5 times damage with their moves
*   Pawns will hold on with 1 HP if they would be knocked out from full health
*   Kings have increased Priority on all their moves
*   Knights deal x3 damage to Queens and x1.25 damage when using moves that target both opponents in a double battle
*   Bishops boost Attack and Special Attack on entry
*   Rooks boost Defense and Special Defense on entry
############################################################################################
(The following moves are affected by this field.):
*   These moves become 'Chess Attacks,' gaining a x1.5 increase in power(Barrage increases x2) and applying additional Rock type damage. 
	Psychic		Strength	Ancient Power		Continental Crush
        Barrage        	Secret Power	Shattered Psyche
*   These moves increase in base power x1.5:
	Fake Out		Feint			Feint Attack	First Impression
        Sucker Punch        	Shadow Sneak		Smart Strike
*   The following moves leave the user susceptible to critical hits:
   	Stomping Tantrum 	Outrage
        Thrash
*   These moves protect from all moves, attacking or otherwise.
        King's Shield       Obstruct
*   These moves increase their normal stat-changing effects:
	Calm Mind	  Nasty Plot
*   King's Shield additionally lowers an attacker's Special Attack 2 stages on contact
*   Trick Room lasts for 8 turns    
*   False Surrender applies taunt.
*   No Retreat now raises Attack, Special Attack and Speed by two stages, but lowers Defense and Special Defense by 1.
*   Nature Power becomes Ancient Power
*   Camouflage changes the user's type to Psychic
*   Terrain Pulse becomes Psychic-type
*   Secret Power may lower Defense
*   Shelter halves damage from Psychic Moves
############################################################################################
(The following abilities are affected by this field):
*   Chess Attacks deal x2 damage to Pokemon with the following abilities:
	Oblivious	Simple		Unaware		Klutz 
        Defeatist
*   Chess Attacks deal x2 damage to Pokemon suffering from Confusion
	(This does not stack with the above abilities)
*   Chess Attacks deal x0.5 damage to Pokemon with the following abilities:
	Adaptability	Synchronize	Anticipation	Telepathy
*   Pokemon with Stance Change boost and lower Attack and Defense one stage when switching between respective forms
*   Merciless gives an increased chance to crit, the lower the HP of the opponent is.

*   Pokemon with Klutz fail at using Chess Attacks.
*   Queenly Majesty Pokemon deal x1.5 damage (does not stack with the Queen piece boost)
*   Illusion Pokemon deal x1.2 damage while Illusion is active.
*   Competitive Pokemon deal more damage the lower their HP is. (linearly, up to 2x) Normal effect is negated.

*   Mimicry changes the users type to Psychic

(The following items are affected by this field):
*       Synthetic Seed boosts Sp.Atk and applies Magic Coat to the user
